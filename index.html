About Us

Village Info Tech, founded in 2023, is a professional organization dedicated to delivering high-quality Medical Coding, Healthcare IT, and Technology Solutions.

Since our inception, our mission has been to simplify healthcare documentation and billing by providing precise, efficient, and compliant coding solutions. We partner with hospitals, clinics, and independent physicians to ensure accuracy, compliance, and smooth revenue cycles.

With a team of certified medical coders and technology experts, Village Info Tech stands out for:

  • ✔ Accuracy and compliance in every medical code
  • ✔ Faster claim approvals and reduced rejections
  • ✔ Customized coding and IT solutions
  • ✔ Global service reach with cost-effective models

We believe in empowering healthcare providers to focus on what truly matters: delivering quality patient care while we take care of coding, billing, and compliance.
